Story:

Beatrix Kiddo aka THE BRIDE is the main protagoist of Quinten Tarantinos Kill Bill. All though her early life is not well known, she starts her story as a former member of the Deadly Viper Assassianation Squad run by her mentor and romantic partner Bill. After finding out that she was pregnant with Bill's baby, she decided to fake her death and leave the life of an assassian and Bill to escape the danger she feared for the life of her unborn baby. The Deadly Viper Assassination Squad entered the chapel, and killed all the attendees. Now alone, Beatrix is brutally beatened almost left for death. Lying on the floor, Bill approarches her with a gun, just as he pulld the trigger,she reveals that the baby is hism but he fires a bullet into her head, she id left for dead. Left in the hospital in an coma she is visited by Elle Driver who is to kill her with a lethal injections however Elle is stoped by a phone call from Bill saying to leave her and let her have a more honorable death. 4 years later she wakes up finding the metal plate in her head where she was gone and that her baby is gone. She escapes the hospital killing the male nurse that used her body for prostitution and one of his clients that tried to rape her. She teaches herself to walk again and escapes in his car names the "Pussy Wagon." She decides to get her revenege and sets out to find and kill The Deadly Viper Assassination Squad and Bill.
